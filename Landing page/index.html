Yoisaki Kanade (宵よい崎さき 奏かなで) is a third-year student taking online classes. She is the leader and composer of the online music circle 25-ji, Nightcord de., going by the alias "K". Though antisocial, Kanade is extremely kind and caring to those close to her (Wife Materials). She often puts the needs of others before herself, overworking herself for the sake of her music. Kanade's entire life is dedicated to composing, often putting off sleep, eating, and going outside to focus on writing music—someone has to be around to look after her (which is me).
